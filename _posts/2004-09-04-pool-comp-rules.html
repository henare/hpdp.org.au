There are a number of issues that continually get raised in relation to the pool comp rules. The most current of these is the way that buybacks are handled. Set rules are required in order that the buyback process is fair to all.The only suggestion we have received to date is from Jo:

  • Anyone arriving BEFORE 20:30 gets a place directly (obviously if
    there are any available)
  • Anyone arriving AFTER 20:30 has no rights at all (unless there
    are loads of spaces)
  • Any first round loser is eligible for a buy-back.
  • The draw should be made when all other first round games have been played (and not before)
