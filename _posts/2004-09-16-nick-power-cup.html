IMPORTANT REMINDER

The Nick Power Cup starts
Wednesday 22nd Sept 7:30pm at Casey's Hotel.

That's next week!

For those of you new to the NPC or just can't remember, read on for the rules

Peter TThe rules are simple:

1. Point are scored over the next 3 Wednesdays (Sep 22 & 29, Oct 6).
At the end of the game, the winner is awarded 8 points while the loser
starts with seven and deducts from that the number of his balls left on the table.

2. The person with the most points at the end of the third
Wed wins the prestigious and coveted Nick Power Cup.

3. After each game you must tell Al the number of balls you sunk
during the game. It is YOUR responsibility to remember to tell Al.
Please make sure that your points plus those of your opponent
only add up to a maximum of 15 points per game.

Could you please ensure that everyone who may not be on the
mailing list or may not check the website, is informed about the NPC.

Good luck to everyone! Let the games begin!

Peter T
