Dear pool friends,

Ringmaster Garth has returned from his fact finding mission in the Eastern block. And finally, I'm sad to have to report that the HPDP slipped from its regular first place in the Trivia to a very disappointing second, however I'm sure it's only a glitch. Don't forget to check the pool ladder at www.hpdp.org.au.
