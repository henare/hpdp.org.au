And Peter clarifies the situation on the scoring system for the NPC...

...And well done, John, re the NPC. However, the NPC scoring system is not as you say.

Basically, it's a point per ball sunk. Therefore, if you win a game it's 8 points. A win is always 8 points regardless of how it was won e.g. premature eblackulation by your opponent still means 8 points for the win even if you sunk no balls. The winner of the comp on the night scores 40 points (32 player comp = 5 games won) or 32 points (16-player = 4 games won).

The loser of any game scores a point per ball sunk. A loser can only ever make 7 points max even if they sunk the black as it was obviously a foul if they lost the game.

Hope that clarifies the situation.

Peter T
